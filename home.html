About us

Royal Enfield is an Indian multinational motorcycle manufacturing company, headquartered in Chennai. The Royal Enfield brand, including its original English heritage, is the oldest global motorcycle brand in continuous production. The company operates manufacturing plants in Chennai in India. The first Royal Enfield motorcycle was built in 1901 by The Enfield Cycle Company of Redditch, Worcestershire, Hunter 350 and many more. Royal Enfield also makes adventurous and offroading motorcycles like the Royal Enfield Himalayan. Their motorcycles are equipped with single-cylinder and twin-cylinder engines.

Our Services

Parts & Accesories

Selling a range of bike parts and accessories, including helmets, lights, locks, pumps, water bottle holders, and more. These accessories can enhance safety, convenience, and yyoverall riding experience.

Rentals

Offering bike rentals for short-term use. This service is ideal for tourists, occasional riders, or those looking to try different bike models before making a purchase.

Insurance

Offering bike insurance plans to cover theft, damage, and accidents. These plans provide peace of mind to bike owners, knowing their investment is protected.

Customization

Customizing bikes to meet individual needs and preferences. This can include custom paint jobs, frame sizes, handlebar styles, and component upgrades

GPS & Tracking Systems

Installing GPS and tracking systems on bikes for enhanced security and navigation. These systems help track the bike's location and provide route guidance.

Eco-Friendly Initiatives

Promoting eco-friendly practices, such as bike recycling programs, eco-friendly products, and supporting sustainability initiatives in the community.
